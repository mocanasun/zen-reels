Privacy Policy

App: Zen Reels  |  Last updated: May 15, 2026

Zen Reels ("the app") is an automated content pipeline that generates short-form inspirational videos and publishes them to TikTok using the TikTok Content Posting API. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Access

Zen Reels requests the following TikTok permissions:

  • user.info.basic — We read your TikTok display name and account ID solely to verify authentication and confirm the correct account is connected.
  • video.upload — We upload pre-generated video files to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • video.publish — We publish uploaded videos to your TikTok profile, either immediately or at a scheduled time you configure.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Your TikTok account data is used only to authenticate API requests and post videos.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third party.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine in an encrypted environment and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • Generated videos and metadata logs are stored locally and are not shared externally.
  • Tokens are automatically refreshed and expire according to TikTok's token lifecycle.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Pexels — We fetch royalty-free nature footage via the Pexels API. No personal user data is shared with Pexels. See Pexels Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok — Videos are published via the TikTok Content Posting API. See TikTok Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain OAuth tokens only as long as necessary to operate the app. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok → Settings & Privacy → Security → Manage app permissions. Upon revocation, all stored tokens are invalidated immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

Zen Reels is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
